About

♦ ♦ ♦

The Avinya Conclave is a first of its kind State Level Intercollegiate Commerce Fest which is being organised by the Department of Commerce and its students. It is a platform for young innovative minds to collaborate and bring in novel ideas and opinions alongside like-minded professionals. The Avinya Conclave with the theme ‘Aagami – Seize the Future’ looks out for different perspectives and intellectual solutions to various challenges put forth & the events are designed with an aim to BRAINSTORM, LEARN, CONNECT & INNOVATE through fostering creativity, problem solving, teamwork and leadership.
